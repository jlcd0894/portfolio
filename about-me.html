About me

Hello stranger, my name is Jose Luis Chavez and I'm a Graphic Designer based in San Diego, CA. I graduated from CETYS University with a BA in Digital Graphic Design Engineering two years ago and I've been working as a designer for almost four years now.

From an early age, I’ve considered myself to be a hard-working and creative thinker, which led me to chose a career in graphic design. This allowed me to further develop my creativity and has given me the chance to push myself to acquire new skills and strategies, which I can apply to a wide range of design projects depending on the client’s needs.

Through my years of working as a designer, I’ve been able to learn new abilities and keep up to date with the latest tech and design trends with the help of face-to-face and online courses (view course certificates).

I’m constantly looking for new ways to learn and challenge myself. That is why, if I don’t know how to do something, I don’t consider it an obstacle, but a learning opportunity to grow and be better.
