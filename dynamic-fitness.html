Dynamic Fitness

The primary objective of this school assignment was to create an fictional business idea and develope its brand identity coroborating with 3 other designers, while working on a very tight schedule to deliver the final product.

This design of this brand required a lot of patience because all 4 members of the team had to agree on every decision, I took it upon myself to be the leader of the project and this helped me develop team building/working skills as well maximizing their strengths and minimizing their weaknesses.

The brand that we created was called Dynamic Fitness, it promised a whole new experience for the it's users, including several amenities that as a whole, we thought would make it the best Gym in Tijuana, the branding included the naming of the company, a logo, responsive webpage, mobile application and a cooporate identity manual.

Tools used:

Challenge

Develop the brand indentity for a fictinoal business that according to the needs of our society, would be succesful.

Strategy and Approach

Create a new fitness experience with all the amenities that a high end gym possess, using research and design to appeal our target audience.

Result

The end result was a synchronized effort from all the team members, as we all had our input on every aspect of the project, we managed to create a unified design for all the final products and give this brand a personality and identity.
