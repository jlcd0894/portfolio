Crowd radar

This project required me to create the brand identity for an iOS app, which included creating a logo, which per the clients request, needed to look like a radar and the letter "C", it was also required a UX/UI that would appeal it's target audience.

For this project I worked directly with an iOS developer, the accesibility and ease of comunication with the client made the design process easier as all the feedback was almost immediate and constructive , this aided me in developing one on one communicating skills.

Whether you're looking for the most popular nightclub, the best taco shop or a calm coffee place, Crowd Radar is the perfect city guide for you. Just open the app and start exploring trendy locations around you.

Download Crowd radar for your iOS devices here!

Challenge

Create an application that helps the user find concurred places nearby with a location based service.

Strategy and Approach

The strategy was to create a service that identifies the amount of people at certain locations and implement a review/comment system to display user generated data.

Result

The final product helps the user identify trending places, interact with other users through the app and leave reviews and comments about these establishments.
